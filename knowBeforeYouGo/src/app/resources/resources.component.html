Water Watcher Tag

Any time children are in the pool, designate an adult to be the Water Watcher. This dedicated observer must commit to actively supervising children in the water at all times; not texting, reading, socializing, drinking, etc.
Download a water watcher tag today or email KBYG@childrens.com to request one be sent to you free-of-charge in the mail.
